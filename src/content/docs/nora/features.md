## Account Isolation

- Multiple accounts with separate cookie storage

This is the main structural feature that makes Nora useful for people who need clean separation between personal, work, creator, or throwaway accounts.

## Blocking And Cleanup

- No ads
- EasyList and EasyPrivacy blocklist support
- Tracking URL query parameter cleanup

## Media And Reading

- Image download
- Video download support for Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and X
- Text selection
- Zoom support

## Customization

- Custom CSS support

## Platform Notes

The app ships on mobile and desktop, but the product idea is the same on every platform: keep the real web service while removing common friction around ads, account juggling, and media handling.
