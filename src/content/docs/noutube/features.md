## Playback

- Ad-free playback
- Background playback
- Original audio playback support
- Live chat support

## Library And History

NouTube includes local management features that do not depend on signing in:

- Watchlist and music library management without login
- Watch history management without login

## Discovery And Feeds

- RSS feed reader for YouTube channels
- Shorts hiding

## Downloads

- Video download support

## Customization

- Custom CSS support

If you want to tune the look and behavior of the embedded sites, continue to [Customization](/noutube/customization/).
